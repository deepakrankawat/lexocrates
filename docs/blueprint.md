# **App Name**: Northman Law

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Displays a compelling headline and subheadline, highlighting the value proposition with a call-to-action.
- Partnership Logos Section: Showcase partner logos to build credibility.
- Services Section: Detail the law firm's expertise with expandable service listings.
- Lawyer Profiles: Displays professional lawyer profile cards to introduce the team.
- Client Testimonials: Feature client testimonials with logos to build trust.
- Blog Section: Display the latest news and blog posts to provide valuable legal insights.
- Contact Information: Showcases contact details including addresses, phone numbers, emails, and hours of operation

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#192A56) evoking trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Off-white (#F5F5DC) providing a clean and elegant backdrop.
- Accent color: Gold (#B8860B) used for highlights and call-to-actions, conveying prestige.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, to provide a sense of elegance, while using 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for the body text.
- Use of simple, professional icons related to legal services, and social media, throughout the website.
- Clean, modern layout with ample whitespace, ensuring a professional and easy-to-navigate interface. Mobile-responsive design.
- Smooth fade-in and slide animations when sections appear on scroll. Subtle hover effects on buttons and cards.
